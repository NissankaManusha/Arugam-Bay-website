Discover Arugam Bay

Located on Sri Lanka's stunning southeast coast, Arugam Bay is renowned for its relaxed vibe, scenic beaches, and world-class surfing. This charming coastal town attracts surfers, backpackers, and beach lovers alike.

Surfing

Cultural Sites

Visit ancient temples and landmarks like Muhudu Maha Viharaya.
